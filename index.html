About

PM work is about decisions and trade-offs

I've spent the last decade in product — biometric tech, fintech fraud, fashion e-commerce, sports apps, and travel platforms. That breadth isn't accidental. Every domain taught me something the others couldn't: what rigour looks like in a regulated environment, how to move fast when the market demands it, how to build community-driven products without losing quality control.

I work with companies serious about getting product right. That usually means they've outgrown gut-feel decisions but haven't built the internal infrastructure to replace them. I help close that gap — with process, frameworks, and experience from places that have already made the mistakes.

Based in Lisbon. Available remotely or on-site across Europe and globally.
