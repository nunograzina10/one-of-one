Services

What I do

Focused engagements, not retainer bloat. Each service is designed to produce something concrete.

01
Product strategy & positioning

Clarify what you're building, for whom, and why it wins. Market framing, competitive analysis, and a north star that guides every decision downstream.

02
Discovery & user research

Structured research to find the problems worth solving. Interview plans, synthesis, and an opportunity space you can act on — not a 40-slide report that gathers dust.

03
Roadmap & prioritisation

Turning competing ideas into a coherent plan. Stakeholder alignment, scoring models grounded in real data, and a roadmap your engineers will actually follow.

04
Interim / embedded PM

Join your team for a defined period — parental leave cover, between hires, or a critical launch — and work exactly as an internal PM would.

05
PM team coaching

Level up a team that's growing faster than its processes. From discovery habits to stakeholder communication, practical skills that stick.

06
AI product integration

Find where AI creates genuine value in your product — not where it looks impressive. Identify the right features, set up the right evals, and ship with confidence.

How it works

Simple process, concrete outputs

Every engagement starts with understanding your actual situation — not my preferred framework.

01
Scoping call (free)

We spend 30 minutes on your situation. I'll be direct about whether I can help and what good help looks like. No commitment required.

02
Proposal with clear deliverables

A written scope, timeline, and fixed or day-rate price. You know exactly what you're buying before signing anything.

03
Focused engagement

I work in your tools, with your team, following your cadence. Weekly check-ins. No surprises.

04
Handoff that lands

Everything documented and transferred so your team can execute without me. I'm available for questions after the engagement closes.

AI tools in every engagement

  • Automated synthesis of user interviews — pattern detection across hundreds of data points in hours, not weeks
  • Competitive intelligence monitoring — continuous, not quarterly
  • Rapid prototyping for early concept validation with real users
  • AI-assisted PRD drafting and spec review
  • Experiment design and metric tracking with predictive modelling

AI speeds up the groundwork. Human judgment decides what to build and why. I use both deliberately.

About

PM work is about people, decisions, and trade-offs

I've spent the last decade in product — across biometric tech, fintech fraud, fashion e-commerce, sports apps, and travel platforms. That breadth is not accidental. Every domain taught me something the others couldn't: what rigour looks like in a regulated environment, how to move fast when the market demands it, how to build community-driven products without losing control of quality.

I work with companies that are serious about getting product right. That usually means they've outgrown gut-feel decisions but haven't yet built the internal infrastructure to replace them. I help close that gap — with process, frameworks, and experience from places that have already made the mistakes.

I'm based in Lisbon. I work with clients across Europe and globally, either remotely or on-site when it matters.
